What is Brintic?

Brintic is a content management system designed to make it simple to maintain your website. It provides a secure admin portal where you can log in, view your pages, and update their content while previewing your changes in real time.

Establishing an online presence for your business shouldn't be difficult. Brintic makes it easy.

Get Started!

We at Brintic pride ourselves on delivering a quality product. Helping people develop their online presence by designing elegant websites brings us great joy. But everyone's needs are different, and that is why we offer two different approaches to using Brintic.

1. We Do Everything

If you want a beautiful website, but aren't a web developer, let us take it from there. We'll work with you to find out exactly what you want and develop a gorgeous website to fit your needs. We'll handle everything that needs to be done to get your site up and running - and when it's live, we'll teach you how to use Brintic to maintain your new website.

Our pricing for this model varies on a case by case basis, so contact us and we will work with you to come up with quote that works for everyone.

2. You Do Everything

Brintic is entirely open source. If you're a developer who wants to use Brintic as the backbone for their next project, please do! The entire project is available on GitHub. Brintic has an MIT license and can be used for free for any project, personal or commercial. All we ask is that you let us know if you build something awesome - we would love to see it.
